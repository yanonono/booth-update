Optimized snail marker 紹介文

Simple snail marker to draw with your finger Allows you to draw with your finger after 'toggling' drawing with fist(more info below). It's also more optimized since the snail marker only draws when it's toggled rather than at all times. The original idea of the snail marker was by https://github.com/theepicsnail/Marker Put the prefab under Right wrist/IndexFinger1_R/IndexFinger2_R/IndexFinger3_R, make sure it's set to (0,0,0) and position TrailSource(important, TrailSource, not Toggle Marker) to be more or less on front your index finger(wherever you position it is where it will start drawing from) Copy the frames from the Fist, FingerPoint and RocknRoll animations into your own Fist, Point and RocknRoll animations Draw by using FingerPoint with your right hand and erase with RocknROll. This needs to be done while having fist "toggled" with your left hand(please watch this short video to see what I mean exactly https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iVnw8NvYAoI&feature=youtu.be ) ------------------------------- Explanation(not needed to know, but in case you're curious): Since fist is analogic, when you go from fist to your hand rest pose it's still considered that you're in fist unless you do open hand or any other gesture so you can sort of think of it as a toggle To draw, "toggle" fist with your left hand and do fingerpoint with your right hand. This way the snail marker won't dissapear even if you stop doing fingerpoint since fist still remains active even if your left hand is in rest pose since like I said before it still consider it fist if you go from fist to rest pose until you do some other hand gesture with your left hand. To erase the ink simply do Thumbsup while having fist toggled I find this pretty useful because it allows you to use fingerpoint without necessarily drawing.
